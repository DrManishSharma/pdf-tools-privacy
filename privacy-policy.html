Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

PDF Tools ("the App") is developed by Perceptron AI and IT Solutions Inc ("we", "us", or "our"). This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

Overview

PDF Tools is designed with privacy as a core principle. The App processes PDF files entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or files.

Data Collection

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • PDF files or their contents
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Browsing or search history

How the App Works

All PDF processing (merging and splitting) is performed locally on your device. Your files are never uploaded to any server or cloud service. The App does not require an internet connection to perform its core functions.

Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • Storage Access (Android 12 and below): Required to read and write PDF files on your device. On Android 13 and above, the App uses the system file picker and does not require broad storage permissions.
  • Internet Access: Used solely for Google Play Billing to process optional in-app donations. No user data is transmitted over the internet.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional donations through Google Play Billing. These transactions are handled entirely by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google processes payment data.

Children's Privacy
